About Us

A trust built on compassion, run with transparency

Vandan Foundation Seva Trust is a registered non-profit organization committed to uplifting underserved communities through sustained action in education, healthcare, nutrition, environment and disaster relief. Every rupee donated and every hour volunteered is tracked and reported back to our community of supporters.

A society where no child is denied education, no family sleeps hungry, and no community is left behind — built through collective, sustained seva.

  • Deliver measurable relief in education, health and nutrition to the most vulnerable
  • Mobilise volunteers and donors into a transparent, accountable network of change
  • Champion environmental stewardship alongside human welfare
  • Expand education support to 2,300+ children by 2024
  • Run 45+ free medical camps annually across three states
  • Plant 1,200+ trees under our green India initiative
  • 100% registered & audited — 12A, 80G and PAN verified
  • On-ground volunteer network across cities and villages
  • Transparent donation tracking and annual impact reports

Leadership

Founder

Founder

Sahil Agja

Founder, Vandan Foundation Seva Trust

Sahil founded Vandan Foundation Seva Trust in 2017 with a single mission — to make dignity accessible to every family, regardless of income or address. Under his leadership, the trust has grown from a small neighbourhood ration drive into a multi-city network of volunteers, donors and partner institutions.

"I believe seva is not charity — it is responsibility. Every person we've served has taught us something about resilience, and every volunteer who has joined us has multiplied our reach. "

— Sahil Agja
